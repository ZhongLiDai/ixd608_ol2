Background Research

Inspiration

Many product design students at the university struggle to find users to interview for their research and to test their products for feedback. To address this issue, I want to create a platform that connects students with users, making it easier to gather valuable information and insights.

User Research

In order to better understand the designer pain points in design process, I started to make research about that.

Qustionnair

User Interview

Insights

1. Designers find it difficult to locate target users to interview.

2. Students cannot find users to test their products, get feedback, and improve their design.

3. People want a quiet space to communicate with users, conduct interviews, and get feedback.

4. Many designers don't know how to ask questions, which can make them seem like strangers.

5. Designers think asking strangers is an inefficient idea, so they want a straightforward way to find target users.

According to these research, I make design analysis.
